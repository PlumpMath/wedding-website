Getting  There

Driving

Peruse the handy map above, or key the following addresses into your sat nav:
For the ceremony, go to All Saints Church – GL6 7BB.
For the reception, go to Beaudesert Park – GL6 9AF.

Parking

All Saints Church - You can park in the playground of Blue Coat Primary School (take the turning by The Bear Inn, onto School Road).  Beaudesert Park School has plenty of parking, and please follow the signs.

Train

The nearest rail station is Stroud, which is 10-15 minutes from both the church and the reception venue.

Taxi

Taxis are available at Stroud station, and can be pre-booked through A&A Taxis (01453 767777) or Five Star Taxis (01453 75 55 11).
